Refund Policy

Introduction. At Optimuu, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. In the rare case of misuse or abuse, this Refund Policy protects our customers. This Policy applies to purchases made on or after the Effective Date.

Eligibility for Refunds

Customers can request a refund for purchases made via the Optimuu platform. Optimuu will determine, in its sole and absolute discretion, if misuse or abuse has occurred.

Exclusions

The following situations are excluded under this Policy:

  • Requesting a refund more than 30 days after purchase.
  • Seeking a refund for purchases made outside the Optimuu platform.
  • Seeking a refund for services already fully provided or digital goods already downloaded.
  • Filing excessive or duplicate claims.

Submitting a Claim

To make a claim under this Policy, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You still own the payment method used for the purchase.
  • You did not in any way contribute to or induce the misuse or abuse.
  • You fully complete the refund request form and comply with all subsequent information requests.

Modification or Termination

Optimuu reserves the right to modify or terminate this Policy at any time, in its sole and absolute discretion, without prior notice.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us at support@optimuu.com.
