Refund Policy

Introduction

At Optimuu, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. In rare cases of misuse or abuse, this Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be granted.

Eligibility for Refunds

Customers may request a refund for purchases made directly through the Optimuu platform. Optimuu reserves the sole and absolute discretion to determine if misuse or abuse has occurred.

Exclusions

The following situations are excluded from this Policy:

  • Requests made more than one (1) year after the purchase date.
  • Requests for refunds of purchases not made on the Optimuu platform.
  • Refund requests for self-initiated purchases.
  • Refund requests for transactions that have already been reversed through chargebacks.
  • Filing excessive or duplicate claims (more than three times in one month).

Submitting a Claim

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You still own the payment method used for the purchase and can receive funds back to that payment method.
  • You did not contribute to, participate in, or induce any misuse or abuse, directly or indirectly.
  • You have completed the required claim form and provided all requested information for evaluation.

By submitting a claim, you agree to cooperate fully with any investigation conducted by Optimuu or relevant authorities.

Processing Refunds

Once a refund is issued, the purchaser will be notified. Personal information provided in the claim process will remain confidential, except as required to process the refund.

Policy Changes

Optimuu reserves the right to modify or terminate this Policy at any time at its sole discretion, without prior notice. Changes will be posted on the Optimuu website.

Final Decision

All refund decisions made by Optimuu are final and at our sole discretion.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us at support@optimuu.com.
